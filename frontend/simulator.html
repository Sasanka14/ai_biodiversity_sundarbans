Data Sources

Our model uses NASA POWER satellite data combined with ground-truth biodiversity observations from the Sundarbans region spanning multiple decades.

Lagged Variables

Ecosystems respond to climate changes over time. Lagged variables capture the delayed effect of temperature and humidity on species populations.

Risk Thresholds

High Risk: Δ ≤ -15 species
