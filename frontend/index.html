Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our conservation AI

How does the AI predict species richness? +

Our neural networks are trained on decades of historical climate patterns and biodiversity indices. The model learns complex relationships between temperature, humidity, and species distribution to forecast shifts in local flora and fauna populations with scientific precision.

Can I export simulation data? +

Yes! All simulations can be exported in CSV or JSON formats, making it easy to share findings with research teams and integrate data into your conservation planning workflows.

What geographic areas does the model cover? +

Currently, our model focuses on the Sundarbans mangrove ecosystem spanning Bangladesh and India. We're expanding to other critical biodiversity zones across Southeast Asia.

How accurate are the predictions? +

Our models achieve 85-92% accuracy on validation datasets. However, predictions represent probabilities and should be used alongside expert ecological judgment for conservation planning decisions.

Can I use this for academic research? +

Absolutely! Our data and models are freely available for academic and non-profit conservation research. Please cite our work and reach out for collaboration opportunities.

How often is the model updated? +

We update our models quarterly with the latest satellite climate data and field observations. Major model improvements are released annually with peer-reviewed validation.
